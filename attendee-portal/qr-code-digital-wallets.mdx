## View QR Code

You can view your event QR codes directly from your **TicketSpot account** for quick check-in at the venue.

**Step 1**: Log in to your **TicketSpot account** and click on the **My Tickets** tab in the top navigation bar.

![my-ticket-tab](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/my-ticket-tab.png)

**Step 2**: You can view all your upcoming events in the Attendee Dashboard. Select the event you purchased a ticket for and click on **View Tickets**.

![upcom-event](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/upcom-event.png)

**Step 3**: Click on the **View QR Code** to see the eventâ€™s QR code.

![btn-view-qr-code](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/btn-view-qr-code.png)

**Step 4**: A **View QR Code** window appears showing your ticketâ€™s QR code. You can scan this code at the event entrance or use the options below to add it to your digital wallet (Google or Apple wallet) for quick access.

![view-qr-code-mod-win](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/view-qr-code-mod-win.png)

> **Note:** Each ticket has a unique QR code. Once scanned at the event check-in, it canâ€™t be reused. Make sure to present the correct QR code for each attendee to avoid access issues.

## Digital Wallet

The **Digital Wallet** feature makes it easier to access your tickets during check-in. After viewing your QR code, you can save the ticket to your mobile wallet for quick and reliable access at the venue â€” even without an internet connection.  

Both Google Pay and Apple Wallet provide offline access to tickets, event reminders, and quick ticket display from your phoneâ€™s lock screen or notifications for quick and easy entry.

### Add to Google Wallet (Android)

1. Click on the **Add to Google Wallet** below your QR code.

![add-to-google-btn](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/add-to-google-btn.png)

2. Review the details of your event and click on **Add** to confirm. This saves your ticket pass to your Google Wallet.

![add-btn-google](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/add-btn-google.png)


Your ticket will appear in the **Google Wallet** app with the event name, QR code, date, and time. You can open it anytime for scanning at the venue entrance.

![ticket-google-wallet](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/ticket-google-wallet.png)

### For iOS (Apple Wallet)
1. Click on the **Add to Apple Wallet** below your QR code.

![add-to-apple-wallet](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/add-to-apple-wallet.png)


2. Review the details of your event and click on **Add to wallet** to confirm. This saves your ticket pass to your Google Wallet.

![ios-to-wallet](../assets/attendee-portal/qr-code-digital-wallets/ios-add-to-wallet.png)


You can view your saved ticket anytime in the **Apple Wallet** app for check-in at the event.
