## Custom Fields
Custom Fields are used to add extra information to your event that isnâ€™t included in the default fields. These fields can store any additional details you need and can also be used in automation workflows or RSVP forms to help you manage event data more effectively.

**How to Add Custom Fields**

**Step 1:** Click the **Create Custom Field** button to start adding a new field for your event.

![click-create-custom-filed-btn](../assets/create-events/tags-and-custom-fields/click-create-custom-filed-btn.png)

A pop-up window will appear where you can create your custom field.

![create-cust-field-modal-win](../assets/create-events/tags-and-custom-fields/create-cust-field-modal-win.png)


**Step 2:** Enter the **Field Name** (the label attendees will see) and the **Field ID** (automatically generated based on the name). 

The Field ID is used for personalization tokens in emails and automation.

**Example:**  
**Field Name**: Enter Your Nickname  
**Field ID**: enter_your_nickname  
Use this ID to personalize your emails: `event_enter_your_nickname`

![field-name-id](../assets/create-events/tags-and-custom-fields/field-name-id.png)

**Step 3:** Click the **Create** button to save your custom field and add it to your event.

![click-create-btn](../assets/create-events/tags-and-custom-fields/click-create-btn.png)

**Step 4:** A new custom field will appear in the list, showing the field name, an input box for attendee responses, and the fieldâ€™s unique ID you can use in emails or automation.

![custom-field-add](../assets/create-events/tags-and-custom-fields/custom-field-add.png)

You can create as many custom fields as you need to collect additional event information.

![multiple-cust-field](../assets/create-events/tags-and-custom-fields/multiple-cust-field.png)

## Manage Custom Fields
